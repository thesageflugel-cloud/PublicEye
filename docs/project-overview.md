# Project Overview

## Title

PublicEye - Smart Civic Grievance Monitoring and Escalation System

## Problem Statement

Most civic complaints are still reported through phone calls, paper notes, or informal channels. These methods make it hard to track ownership, resolution progress, or department performance. Citizens do not know whether their complaint is being acted upon, and authorities lack a unified dashboard for prioritization and accountability.

## Proposed Solution

PublicEye provides a structured digital grievance platform where:

- Citizens submit complaints with description, category, priority, location, and image evidence.
- Administrators assign cases to departments and officers.
- Officers update progress through a controlled workflow.
- The system highlights overdue cases using SLA-based escalation levels.
- Dashboards provide visibility into complaint volume, resolution speed, and department efficiency.

## Major Modules

- Authentication and role management
- Complaint intake and evidence upload
- Complaint workflow and escalation engine
- Officer and admin dashboards
- Activity timeline and auditability
- Department performance analytics

## Key Engineering Value

- Demonstrates RBAC and access control
- Shows workflow-driven system design
- Includes persistence, validation, and audit trails
- Supports interview discussion on scalability and smart governance systems
